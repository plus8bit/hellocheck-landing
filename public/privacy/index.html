Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-11-15

This Privacy Policy describes how HelloCheck (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses and protects information when you use our website and the HelloCheck Telegram bot (@HelloCheckAppBot).

1. What data we collect

  • Receipt photos that you send to the bot.
  • Telegram user ID and basic profile info (username / name).
  • Usage data such as timestamps of scans and basic technical logs.

2. How we use the data

  • To analyse your receipts and generate nutrition insights.
  • To improve the quality and reliability of the service.
  • To prevent abuse and ensure security.

3. AI and third-party services

We use third-party AI providers to process receipt images and generate nutrition analysis. Your data may be temporarily processed on their servers to provide the service. We do not sell your data.

4. Data retention

Receipt data may be stored for a limited time to provide history inside the bot and to improve the service. You can request deletion of your data by contacting us.

5. Security

We use reasonable technical measures to protect your data, but no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

6. Children

HelloCheck is not intended for children under 16. If you believe a child has used the service, please contact us.

7. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version is always available on this page.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at hello@hellocheck.app.
